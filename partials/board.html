Attention Election Board Workers

Documentation PDFs for the Philadelphia City Commissioners' new Poll Pads and Voting System are now available!

Poll Pads:

The Poll Pad® by KnowInk will replace the old paper poll books. Manual topics broadly cover:

  • Poll worker check-in
  • Opening Procedures

  • Closing Procedures
Voting System:

The ExpressVote XL Ballot Marking Device by ES&S will replace the old DRE voting machines. Manual topics broadly cover:

  • Understanding the ExpressVote XL
  • Opening the polls
  • Voting Procedures
  • Closing the polls
  • Troubleshooting
