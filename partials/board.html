Attention Election Board Workers

Documentation PDFs for the Philadelphia City Commissioners' new Poll Pads and Voting System are now available!

Poll Pads:

The Poll Pad® by KnowInk has replaced the outdated and inefficient paper model that is often the cause of long lines at the polls and inefficient election record keeping. Touch screens & on-device search bring ease of use. Manual topics broadly cover:

  • Opening Procedures
  • Processing Voters
  • Closing Procedures
Voting System:

The ExpressVote XL™ Universal Voting System by ES&S is a full-face, 32" touch screen ballot. It captures a voter's choices by marking a paper ballot while also tabulating the vote data for later collection and reporting. Manual topics broadly cover:

  • Understanding the ExpressVote XL
  • Usage
  • Troubleshooting
