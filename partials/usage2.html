1 - Get Your Paper Ballot

Go to your polling place, check-in, and receive your paper ballot from the poll worker.

2 - Initiate the Voting Session

Insert the paper ballot into the ballot feeder with the notch in and to the right.

3 - Make Selections

Using the screen, touch the candidate of your choice or write-in a desired candidate.

4 - Print Your Ballot

Touch PRINT and your selections will print out in the window on the right.

5 - Verify Your Vote

In the window on the right, review and verify your selections.

6 - Cast Your Vote

Press VOTE and your ballot is scanned, tabulated, and stored in the secure ballot container.
